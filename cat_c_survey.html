Column Definitions
Score
Priority score (0–1) combining Gaia G brightness (60%) and absolute galactic latitude (40%). Brighter Gaia sources missing from ZTF are scored higher.
Gaia G
Mean apparent magnitude in the Gaia G band (330–1050 nm).
ZTF cat sep
Angular separation (arcsec) to the nearest ZTF catalog source within 2″. Sources within 2″ with consistent brightness were already removed as false positives.
ZTF cat mag
Mean magnitude of the nearest ZTF catalog source.
|Δmag|
Absolute magnitude difference between Gaia G and the nearest ZTF catalog source.

About Category C

Category C sources are Gaia objects with no ZTF counterpart within 1.0″. They could be very red or faint sources below ZTF detection limits, sources in ZTF chip gaps, or transients that faded between Gaia and ZTF observations.

Sources brighter than 14.5 mag (Gaia G) are excluded due to ZTF saturation. Known Gaia variables and soft-match false positives (ZTF source within 2″ with |Δmag| < 2) are also removed.
